Know Your Net

Know Your Net is an educational podcast designed to demystify the complexities of the internet, exploring how it shapes our daily lives, societal norms, and individual behavior. Each episode provides listeners with valuable insights into the hidden mechanics of the digital world, from algorithms and privacy concerns to the influence of social media and cybersecurity practices.

As a game designer and educator, I am passionate about empowering individuals through knowledge and fostering critical thinking about digital environments. Know Your Net was born from my commitment to bridging the gap between technological literacy and everyday life. The podcast has proven to be a resource for both students and the general public, allowing listeners to better understand and navigate the digital landscape. My role as the producer and host has expanded my ability to engage audiences beyond the realm of interactive media, showcasing my versatility in educational outreach.

Insights to Incite

Insights to Incite is a thought-provoking podcast that amplifies voices from various disciplines to inspire action and spark positive change. The podcast brings together conversations with industry leaders, educators, and changemakers who share their journeys, insights, and lessons learned in their respective fields. Each episode is curated to motivate listeners and challenge them to think critically about their own impact.

Producing Insights to Incite aligns with my core belief that stories and shared experiences are powerful tools for personal and collective growth. This podcast is a platform where education meets inspiration, reflecting my ongoing commitment to nurturing a culture of learning and dialogue. Through this project, I have had the privilege of facilitating discussions that resonate with both students and professionals seeking to make meaningful contributions in their fields. My dual role as a producer and host has allowed me to refine my storytelling abilities and strengthen my engagement strategies, skills that are invaluable in my work as an educator and creative professional.

Both Know Your Net and Insights to Incite exemplify my dedication to using media as a tool for education and awareness. These projects showcase my ability to produce and host content that informs, inspires, and sparks conversations, whether about technology's role in society or the paths people take to create change. These podcasts highlight my adaptability and the breadth of my commitment to fostering understanding, whether through games, interactive media, or audio storytelling.
