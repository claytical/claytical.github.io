A Long Way from Home: The Game

I'm working with Matthew Jacobson and Gaspar Gonzalez to create an interactive experience based on their documentary A Long Way From Home: The Untold Story of Baseball's Desegregation.

Moonlight Flight

The spiritual sequel to my first approved game in the App Store, Moonlight Flight is a physics based puzzle game where players draw lines and tilt the device in order to guide a ball around the screen. Players receive points for destroying certain bumper types while avoiding hazardous obstacles. It can be played slowly, devising a set of lines drawn before a ball is dropped or in real time, drawing lines after seeing physical reactions.
