Hello there!

I'm Clay Ewing, a creative technologist and educator focused on social impact projects. I work at the University of Miami where I teach in the Interactive Media Program. I'm also the Co-Director of NERDLab, where I work with students and other faculty on designing and developing awesome stuff.

The following portfolio pieces have been selected as work that I believe represent my best work:

Dreamy

A dating simulator to change a person's intent to use a tanning bed.

Atlantis

A massively local multiplayer game.

Unsavory

An activist game on paid sick leave for restaurant workers.

Sweatshop Superstar

A brutal rhythm action game that takes a dark comedic look at sweatshops.

Climate Anxiety

A sensor based narrative that uses brain waves to navigate through a story.
